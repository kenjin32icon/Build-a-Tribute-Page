Albert Einstein

The Father of Modern Physics

Albert Einstein photographed by F. Schmutzer in 1921, the year he received the Nobel Prize in Physics.

The Life of a Genius

  • 1879 - Born in Ulm, Germany
  • 1905 - Published his Special Theory of Relativity
  • 1915 - Completed his General Theory of Relativity
  • 1921 - Awarded the Nobel Prize in Physics
  • 1933 - Moved to the United States after fleeing Nazi Germany
  • 1939 - Wrote to President Roosevelt about the potential development of an atomic bomb
  • 1955 - Passed away in Princeton, New Jersey
